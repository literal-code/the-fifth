Welcome to Retro-Igry. This is a site dedicated to retro games from the early 1980s to the late 1990s. Here you can find information and backstories to your favorite games, the impact they had on their respective genres and much more.

Sonic The Hedgehog, released by Sega in 1991, revolutionized platformers with its lightning-fast speed and momentum-based gameplay. Sonic's iconic blue character quickly became Sega's beloved mascot, rivaling Mario in popularity. The game's spin dash move and intricate level designs filled with loops and ramps added a dynamic element to the genre. Sonic's success led to numerous sequels, spin-offs, and a cultural impact that can still be felt today, inspiring modern platformers and solidifying its place as a legendary franchise. From its vibrant aesthetics to its rebellious attitude, Sonic The Hedgehog left an indelible mark on the platformer genre.
